Medical Education & Clinical Excellence

Wilda Pierre, DNP, APRN is a doctorally prepared nurse practitioner and medical educator specializing in clinical education, nursing instruction, and healthcare leadership. Based in Miami, Florida, Wilda provides evidence-based medical education and professional training for nurses, healthcare organizations, and academic institutions.

Medical Education Services

Medical education services focus on strengthening clinical judgment, improving patient outcomes, and supporting healthcare professionals across all stages of their careers. Dr. Pierre’s approach integrates real-world clinical experience with academic rigor, ensuring that education is both practical and evidence-driven.

  • Clinical education and hospital-based training
  • Nursing education for RN, BSN, MSN, and DNP learners
  • Competency-based skills validation
  • Professional development for healthcare teams

Clinical Training & Professional Development

Clinical training programs are designed to support nurses and advanced practice providers in transitioning confidently into complex clinical roles. Dr. Pierre emphasizes clinical excellence, leadership development, and ethical patient care.

  • RN to APRN transition support
  • NP clinical preceptorship and supervision
  • Healthcare leadership education
  • Mentorship for early-career clinicians

Healthcare Leadership & Speaking

In addition to clinical instruction, Wilda Pierre provides professional speaking and educational workshops for healthcare organizations, nursing programs, and conferences. Topics include clinical excellence, leadership development, burnout prevention, and professional identity in nursing.

Serving Miami & Beyond

While based in Miami, Florida, Dr. Pierre offers both in-person and virtual medical education services, allowing organizations nationwide to benefit from her expertise in nursing education and clinical excellence.
